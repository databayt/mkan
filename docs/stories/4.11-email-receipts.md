# Story 4.11: Email receipts via Resend

> **Epic**: 4 · **Status**: To Do · **Phase**: C

## Story
As a guest, I want a confirmation email with my receipt after every successful payment, so that I have a record.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. After Payment status flips to `PAID` (Stripe webhook OR admin verify OR cash check-in), system sends email.
2. Email shows: booking ID, listing/trip details, amount, payment method, dates, host/operator contact.
3. Templates in `src/lib/mail-templates/`.
4. Both en + ar email versions based on user locale.

## Files
- `src/lib/mail.ts`, new templates `src/lib/mail-templates/booking-receipt.tsx`.

## Tests
- Unit: template render snapshot in en + ar.

## Definition of Done
- [ ] AC met · email triggers in both verticals
