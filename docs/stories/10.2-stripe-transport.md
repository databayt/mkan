# Story 10.2: Stripe payment intent for transport

> **Epic**: 10 · **Status**: To Do · **Phase**: D

## Story
As a passenger, I want to pay for a bus seat via Stripe, so that my international card works.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. `processStripe({ bookingId })` creates a Stripe payment intent with `metadata.kind = 'transport_booking'`.
2. Webhook (Story 4.3) dispatches to transport handler when `metadata.kind` matches.
3. Booking auto-confirms on `payment_intent.succeeded`.
